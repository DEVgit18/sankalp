About SANKALP

An organization at BITS Hyderabad that provides a platform for debates, research and conversations on varied subjects important to students and our country. It also includes various wings like the technical, outreach, editorial and research. Our aim is to foster critical thinking and encourage students to engage with pressing issues through discussions. We also aim to provide a space for students to develop their skills in research, programming and public speaking.
